WHAT TO SUBMIT

A demo video of no more than 5 minutes. Your video should cover:

• The problem you set out to solve and who it affects
• A live walkthrough of what you built — show the product working, not slides
• Who would use this and how it could reach them
• What you would build next if you had more time

Keep it focused and move quickly — judges will be watching many videos and a clear, functional demo will always stand out over a polished presentation.

HOW TO SUBMIT

Please use this form to submit your final demo video

Deadline: midnight on Sunday, April 26. No extensions will be granted. 

Only one submission per team

HOW YOU WILL BE JUDGED

All submissions will be scored on the same four criteria, for a total of 100 points:

• Problem Clarity (20 pts) — Is the problem real, specific, and clearly articulated? Do you demonstrate that you understand who faces this problem and why?

• Solution Quality (25 pts) — Does what you built actually address the problem? Is it functional? A working MVP with one solid feature is stronger than an ambitious concept with nothing to show.

• Viability (25 pts) — Could this realistically reach and serve users — whether as a business, a tool, or a public good? Is there a plausible path to getting this to people?

• Execution (30 pts) — How much did you actually build in one week? Did you make smart choices about what to build first? This is the highest-weighted criterion — Build Week is about building.

Judges understand this was built in one week under real constraints. They will judge accordingly.


WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

Thursday, April 30: Top 20 teams are invited for a live 5–10 minute interview with the Ibtikar team
Monday, May 4: Demo Day, top 10 teams pitch live to a panel of judges in an online public event

Only teams that submit a video by the deadline will be considered for Demo Day.
